Shipping and Handling

We offer worldwide shipping

We offer 1 year congenital Health Guarantee Written and sign by both parties Your parrots will be flown in an Airline-Appoved Pet Carrier (with food & water) and always in a temperature and pressure-controlled environment. We ship your parrots in first-class comfort! we strive to provide you with extraordinary customer service from beginning to end.Our experienced staff brings years of knowledge in the parrot shipping industry and can help assist in making your shipping experience as pleasant as possible. We will also explain every detail thoroughly to assure you of your pets safety throughout the shipping process.(We only ship parrots on airlines that implement a Pet Safe Program)

Once you have completed the adoption process, we will coordinate the best time and date to fly your parrots to your nearest, major airport,you will receive an email and/or phone call letting you know how everything is going on with your parrots flight details and our Parrot Care Guide. your parrots would be delivered at your doorsteps by a well trained delivery agent,

A comfortable traveling experience for your animal through temperature-controlled flights and delivery vans and holding areas in select stations to prevent exposure to extreme heat or cold temperatures. Unparalleled customer service from a dedicated team of professionals who will help ensure a seamless journey for your parrots. Specially trained ground handlers for personalized care on the go.

Our parrot shipping service has been developed to make certain that every possible detail is covered when providing transportation of your parrot. Once you have chosen your new family member you want to be certain that they arrive safely into you and your families loving arms. The goal is to provide you with the best experience when adopting your pet. We know patience is limited when you are waiting to meet the newest member of your family so we strive to make your shipping experience quick and easy.

We ship all over USA/CANADA for free to all our clients

Every parrot we ship is handled with the utmost compassion by one of our Shipping Specialists.
